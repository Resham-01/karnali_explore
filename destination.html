Discover Nepal's Wonders

Experience the heart of Nepal.

From the majestic Himalayas to vibrant festivals, Nepal offers a unique blend of culture, adventure, and breathtaking landscapes that will leave you spellbound.

Popular Destinations in Karnali Province

Bheri Bridge

A stunning suspension bridge over the Bheri River, perfect for nature lovers and photography enthusiasts.

Deauti Bajai Temple

A sacred Hindu temple known for its spiritual significance and peaceful ambiance.

Bulbule Lake

A tranquil lake with crystal-clear water surrounded by lush greenery, ideal for picnics and relaxation.

Kakribihar Temple

A historical Buddhist monastery reflecting ancient architecture and cultural heritage.

Ghantaghar

The iconic clock tower at the heart of Surkhet, a must-visit landmark with local charm.

Sahid Park

A beautifully landscaped park dedicated to martyrs, offering serene walks and relaxation spots.

Rara Lake

Known as the "Queen of Lakes," Rara Lake is the largest freshwater lake in Nepal, surrounded by lush forests and snow-capped peaks.

Shey Phoksundo Lake

A pristine turquoise lake located in Dolpo, surrounded by cliffs and known for its unique natural beauty and cultural significance.

Chure Mountain

Part of the Siwalik Hills, this range is ideal for hiking and offers panoramic views of the surrounding valleys and rivers.

Karnali River

The longest river in Nepal, Karnali is famous for white-water rafting, fishing, and exploring the untouched wilderness.
